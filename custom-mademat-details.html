Custom-made coconut coir mats are durable, eco-friendly entrance solutions crafted from the natural, fibrous husks of coconuts. These mats are highly personalized, featuring tailored sizes, shapes, and custom designs such as logos, monograms, or personalized messages for both residential and commercial use.


Material: 100% natural coconut husk fibers (coir) that are biodegradable and sustainable. Texture: Tough, stiff, and abrasive bristles that provide superior scraping action to remove dirt, mud, and debris from shoes. Construction: Typically constructed by weaving fibers or tufting them onto a backing, often with a durable PVC or rubber backing to prevent slipping and improve longevity. Customization: Can be custom-cut to specific dimensions, including for mat wells (recessed areas), and are available in various thicknesses ranging from 14mm to 30mm. Design Options: Custom logos can be created through inlays (cutting and piecing together colored coir) or through high-resolution stenciling/printing with fade-resistant dyes. Appearance: Features a natural beige, brown, or golden color that provides a rustic, warm aesthetic.
