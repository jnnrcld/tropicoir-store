Small kitchen item designed to safely hold and organize eggs. It keeps eggs stable and prevents them from rolling or breaking during storage or serving. Egg holders are commonly made from materials such as plastic, ceramic, metal, or wood and may come in different designs, from simple trays that fit inside refrigerators to decorative holders used for serving boiled eggs at the table. By keeping eggs neatly arranged, an egg holder helps maintain cleanliness, saves space, and makes eggs easier to handle when cooking or preparing meals.
