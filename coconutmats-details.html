Mats

Floor mats made from natural fibers extracted from coconut husks, commonly known as coir. These mats are durable, eco-friendly, and designed to trap dirt, dust, and moisture from shoes before entering a home or building. Because coir fibers are strong and rough, coconut mats are effective for scraping off mud and debris, making them ideal for use as doormats or outdoor mats. They are also biodegradable and sustainable, which makes them an environmentally friendly alternative to synthetic mats. Coconut-based mats are commonly used in homes, offices, and commercial spaces due to their durability, natural appearance, and practical cleaning function.
